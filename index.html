Teach the new generation about human nature through nature itself

At Get out of town, we are dedicated to connecting younger generations with the natural world, fostering environmental stewardship, and inspiring a lifelong love for the outdoors.

Our Goal

Our mission is to empower youth to explore, appreciate, and protect nature by providing opportunities to engage with the outdoors in meaningful ways. We strive to make nature accessible, impactful, and a central part of youth development.
